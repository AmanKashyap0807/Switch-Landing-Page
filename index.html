About Switch

Switch is the first-of-its-kind social app designed exclusively for campuses. Unlike crowded mainstream platforms, switch is a verified, students-only community, safe, authentic and meaningful.

On Switch, you choose how you show up:

As Yourself

Build a profile with your real name, branch, year, hobbies, and photo

Incognito Mode

Pick an avatar + your vibe. Here you have a different name for everyone you talk to. So complete anonymity is assured.

Why Switch is Different:

  • Limited slots to avoid clutter, every connection feels meaningful
  • Combines the best of popular platforms:
    • Matching of Tinder
    • Lifestyle showcase of Instagram
    • Streaks and exclusivity of Snapchat
    • Anonymous fun of Omegle
    • Verified student community like LinkedIn
  • No strangers, no spam, no risk, only real students from your campus exclusively
  • Switch mode: one tap and instantly become an anonymous avatar where you have different name for everyone you talk to
